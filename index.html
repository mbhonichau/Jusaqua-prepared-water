About

Who we are

Jusaqua Prepared Water is a dedicated water purification company serving Makhanda and its neighboring towns.

  • Provide clean, safe drinking water that meets the highest health standards.
  • Free doorstep delivery to our customers, ensuring easy access to purified water.
  • Supporting local businesses and fostering community growth.

In addition to serving individual consumers, we supply large volumes of water to fellow business owners who resell to their customers in residential areas on the outskirts of the city center. This dual approach allows us to reach a broader market and support local businesses.
